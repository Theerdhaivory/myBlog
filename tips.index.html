tips to manage studies

1.love what you study

no matter how much you work,all that matters is your love for what you do.Imagine what it would be like to study sincerely for 6 years and sacrificing your free time.

2.take breaks in between

Do not study continously for 2 hours.Take breaks of 5 minutes and do what you love to do.It will cause unneccessary stress.

3.organize your stuff

remember that you are not a robot to remember everything!Make to-do lists so that you do not need to regret during the deadline

4.take help

If you don't understand anything,than ask your teachers wthout any shyness.They are there to help you with your .Atleast ask your friends,they might explain it better.This tip was so helpful to me

5.Do not mug up everything

It's important that you understand the concept properly.Don't just mug it up.If you understand,then it will be easier for you to remember.
